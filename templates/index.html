You can track your calorie intake using our intuitive calorie tracker feature. Simply log in to your account, enter the foods you consume, and the tracker will automatically calculate your total calorie intake.

Yes, you can customize your fitness goals based on your preferences and objectives. Our platform allows you to set specific calorie targets, track your progress, and adjust your goals accordingly.
